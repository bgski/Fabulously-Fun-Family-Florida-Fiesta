### ✅ Option A: Dinner + Dueling Pianos on I-Drive

Head to I-Drive for dinner near Howl at the Moon. Plenty of solid restaurants within walking distance so you only park once. Howl at the Moon runs **Ladies Night** on Tuesdays — free cover for the ladies, cheap drinks for everyone.

#### Dinner (~1–1.5 hrs)
All within walking distance of Howl at the Moon:

- **Yard House** — American, huge beer selection, shareable plates *(~$20–35)*
- **Tin Roof** — Southern comfort, live music, craft drinks *(~$20–35)*
- **Tapa Toro** — Spanish tapas, paella, flamenco dancers *(~$25–40)*
- **Bar Louie** — Gastropub, craft cocktails, flatbreads *(~$18–30)*
- **Ocean Prime** — Upscale seafood & steaks *(~$50–80)*

#### Howl at the Moon (~2–3 hrs)

- **Address:** 8815 International Drive, Orlando, FL 32819
- **Phone:** (407) 354-5999
- **Hours:** Doors 7 PM · Show 8 PM · Open until 1:45 AM
- **Tuesday Special:** Ladies Night — free cover for ladies, $5 martinis, $3 Bacardi drinks, $12 domestic beer buckets, half-off power hour (7–8 PM)
- **Cover:** ~$5/person (free for ladies)
- **Age:** 21+ only
- **Seating:** First come or reserve online
- **Tip:** Bring $1 and $5 bills for song requests
- **Vibe:** High-energy dueling pianos, sing-alongs, dance-offs, 86-oz bucket drinks

> **Why Tuesday works:** I-Drive won’t be wall-to-wall packed. Ladies Night keeps cover free for half the group, and power hour makes early rounds cheap.

## Wednesday, Feb 18 — Universal Day

### Universal Epic Universe *(~6 hrs at park)*

Universal Orlando now has **three theme parks**. Epic Universe includes Super Nintendo World, Isle of Berk, Dark Universe, and the Ministry of Magic.

#### Ticket Options
- **1-Day Epic Universe Only:** ~$139–200
- **1-Day Park-to-Park:** ~$185–260 (Epic + one other park)

> **Mardi Gras Note:** Universal Mardi Gras runs Feb 7–Apr 4. Park-to-Park lets you catch the parade and food booths in the evening.

#### In-Park Food
- **Toadstool Cafe** — Super Nintendo World · Themed entrées *(~$18–25)*
